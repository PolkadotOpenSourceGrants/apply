# Final Report: Polkadot Open Source Grants Bounty

This document provides a short closing summary of the Polkadot Open Source Grants Bounty based on the public record in:

- [PolkadotOpenSourceGrants](https://github.com/PolkadotOpenSourceGrants/)
- [`PolkadotOpenSourceGrants/apply`](https://github.com/PolkadotOpenSourceGrants/apply)
- [`PolkadotOpenSourceGrants/delivery`](https://github.com/PolkadotOpenSourceGrants/delivery/)

## Summary

The Polkadot Open Source Grants Bounty approved **10 projects** with a combined value of **$248,000 USD**.

Of those approved projects, **8 were successfully completed, delivered, and paid in full**.

## High-Level Statistics

- **Project proposals submitted:** 59
- **Approved projects:** 10
- **Successfully finalized projects:** 8
- **Total approved value:** **$248,000 USD**
- **Successfully delivered and fully paid value:** **$188,000 USD**
- **Additional paid milestone value:** **$10,000 USD**
- **Curator compensation:** **$22,500 USD**
- **Total paid out by the program:** **$220,500 USD**

This means:

- approximately **16.9%** of submitted proposals were approved
- **80%** of approved projects were completed successfully

## Closing Note

The Polkadot Open Source Grants Bounty supported a focused set of open-source projects across the Polkadot ecosystem, including governance tooling, developer SDKs, wallet testing infrastructure, developer tooling, and cross-chain infrastructure. In total, **8 projects were completed successfully**, representing **$188,000 USD** in delivered and fully paid grant value. In addition, the program paid **$10,000 USD** for an approved milestone outside the fully finalized set of projects, and **$22,500 USD** in curator compensation, bringing the total paid out by the program to **$220,500 USD**.

The remaining **427.01 DOT** available in the bounty will be returned to the Polkadot Treasury through a referenda to be submitted on OpenGov.
